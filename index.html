Awlwyn S. Howell

Sept. 7, 1933 - Nov. 25, 2022

My dad worked at JFK airport for 30+ years before he retired. He was the breadwinner, had all the finance under control in the household. My dad also was a handy man as well. Everytime when something go wrong with the house, he'll resolve the issue before calling someone which he rarely did. What my dad would always do on the side as a side hustle was fix people cars in front our house or in our backyard, even during cold weather. Mostly every Saturdays when he's off from work, my dad would have his get togethers among his friends to play cards, drink and watch TV. And on Sundays he go to church or listen to his spiritual radio station all day long while at home. And after his retirement, mostly everyday he'll have his get togethers as he lived out his life until he was unable to due to his health struggles. Now that my dad is gone, the household would never be the same again, and of course no one can't replace him. But I'm sure he's in heaven with the rest of his friends that done passed on doing what he usually does when he get together with them. He'll be miss always.
